Top 5 realistic ways to make money blogging

I'm just going to be talking about the 5 ways that my blog actually makes money and also the three things that I don't do I've been blogging for about eight years at this point but for the first five years or so I did not make a dime for my blog it's only been in about the last three years that my blog actually started getting traffic and started making money but at this point of working at actually monetizing it for about three years my blog does produce enough income to support my family full-time.

1) EXTRA YOUTUBE AD REVENUE: (make money from blogging) :

Alright so the very first way that my blog makes money is by directing people back to my youtube channel which means that my videos get more views and I get more ad revenue.

Now obviously this is a really indirect way that my blog makes money but it definitely adds a few hundred dollars to my income every single month.

2) LOVE WORKSHOPS: (make money from blogging) :

The second way that I make money with my blog is by offering live workshops almost every single month these workshops are typically about 90 minutes long and in them I teach my blog readers something that I know they're interested in because I publish a blog post every single week I can then go back and look at my analytics and see which blog post my readers were most interested in and which one got the most traffic and then what I do is I announce that I'm going to teach an entire workshop on that topic and I give people a link where they can sign up.

I typically charge anywhere between about twenty nine dollars and a hundred dollars for people to take part in these workshops.

3) courses: (make money from blogging) :

The third way that my blog makes money is a lot like the second way because all I do for this third way is I take those workshops that I have already taught and I have recorded and I edit them into individual lessons and I turn them into an online course I typically sell these courses for a bit more money because they have been edited down and I normally add some additional resources to them like workbooks or PDF guides to make them a more complete package.

The great thing about selling these as courses is it produces completely passive income which means that after I finished editing the course and I put it on my website I don't have to do anything else and I make money every single month from people going back and buying those courses that I've created in the past.

4) membership site: (make money from blogging) :

The fourth way that my blog makes money produces the biggest amount of income every month and that is through my membership website called startup Society people who read my blog sign up for my mailing list and then after they're on my mailing list I tell them about startup Society and twice every year I open up start-up society to new members.

Startup Society is a membership program that I host on my blog that gives people access to a whole suite of resources including all my courses and one member exclusive course that is comprehensive course all about how to start and run a successful online business like I mentioned startup Society is currently the biggest chunk of my monthly income at this point we have about 300 members inside and they each pay $33 per month.

5) COACHING AND CONSULTING: (make money from blogging) :

The fifth and final way that I make money with my blog is providing coaching and consulting to my readers professionally I'm a business marketing strategist and I teach people how to start successful profitable online businesses.

So, a lot of my blog readers are interested in starting their own blog or their own YouTube channel or some other type of online business so they hire me to teach them how to do that and to work with them to build a successful online business together.

This consulting work is very profitable but it does require me to invest my time so I am trading hours for dollars as people say so I limit myself to only working with about five or six clients at a time because I really want to be able to provide them the best results and not take up all of my time so that I can keep running my blog and running my youtube channel and growing my brand online.

So, that summarizes the five ways that my blog does make money

Now I wanted to talk about three ways that I do not make money with my blog.

1) Ebooks:

The first way, I do not make money with my blog is with ebooks and the reason that I don't do this I guess there's two reasons first of all because be books don't sell for very much money I could normally package that same information just differently and sell it as a workshop or a course and make anywhere between 30 and 300 dollars per sale but if I package it as an e-book then it's only gonna sell for you know somewhere between ten and thirty dollars at the most.

so, it just wouldn't be very profitable and what makes a lot more sense to me is to create great quality short ebooks and then give them away for free to use to grow my email list so if you're on my blog and you look in the menu you'll see a link that says free resources and if you click on that you'll see a collection of ebooks that you can download for free if you sign it for my email newsletter subscription.

2) ADS:

The second way that my blog does not make money is with ads and specifically Google Adwords now I have nothing against Google AdWords it's a great service for certain types of things but I don't think that it's a good way to make money with your blog and the main reason is because I think that putting these ads on your site really clutters up your blog and makes it not look attractive which means that you're gonna not be able to compete with other blogs that look nicer than yours and blogging is really competitive.

So, you definitely don't want to do something that's gonna make your blog less appealing or less helpful to your readers the other reason that I don't put the ads on my site is because they don't pay very much people these days are really you know oversaturated by advertising they see ads everywhere they go so people don't click on them very much which means you need to have tens of thousands if not hundreds of thousands of visitors to your website every and there's many other ways that you can make a lot more money rather than using advertising which makes your website look ugly and is sending people away from your blog which is the opposite of what you want to do.

3) sponsorships:
