Now here's what you're not gonna find on this list you're not gonna find anything that pays less than about $10 per hour definitely no surveys or searching or watching videos or doing tiny little tasks to make a few pennies here or a few pennies there I don't want you to be wasting your time so I'm gonna be sharing with you 10 jobs that actually pay real good money.

1) video editor :

Now the first job on my list is that of video editor now I used to edit my own videos but for the past year or so I've been working with a video editor and let me just tell you it can be really tough to find a good video editor there's a lot less competition online than you might think for video editors that actually know what they're doing as a video editor you can earn anywhere from about 15 dollars an hour all the way up to about a hundred dollars per hour depending on how advanced your skills are and how well you market your services.

2) course creator:

The next side hustle on my list is that of course creator now of course Creator is someone who creates online courses so they typically are filming videos and writing lessons and designing assignments and as a course creator one of your biggest jobs is actually just marketing your course so most course creators choose to go the route of building up an audience by running a YouTube channel or a blog or something like that and then once they have an audience they create a course to solve a problem that their audience is facing.

Online courses sell for anywhere from about 25 dollars all the way up to two or three thousand dollars personally I sell my courses for between 100 and 1000 dollars but there's obviously a wide range here.

If you are selling on a platform like UDEMY then typically the prices are a lot lower closer to that 25-30 dollar mark whereas if you're selling courses to your own audience and you are working hard on your marketing then you can typically sell them before several hundred if not a few thousand dollars.

3) YouTube ad strategist :

Alright next job YouTube ad strategist so you might have heard of a Facebook ad manager before this is someone who manages the ads that run on Facebook and they're managing them for clients that are businesses that want to advertise on Facebook however I've been noticing a trend lately there are a lot of Facebook ad strategists out there and Facebook as a platform is on the decline now that does not mean that you cannot still be a successful Facebook ad strategist there's still millions and millions of people on Facebook tons of businesses are still running ads there I just noticed that the field is getting a little bit saturated which is why on this list I'm recommending being a YouTube ad strategist.

Right now there's this special situation where YouTube ads are kind of on sale and that's because of a big YouTube advertiser crisis you may or may not know about but long story short about a year ago a bunch of YouTube's biggest advertisers had a little bit of a hissy fit and kind of all ran away.

YouTube ads strategist generally earn about fifty to two hundred dollars per billable client hour however they generally are charging their clients which are small businesses buy the package so they'll charge them a certain amount to set up the ads or a certain amount per month to manage the ads.

4) Public Notary :

Let's move on to the next side hustle opportunity on my list and that is a public notary now public notaries and loan signing agents do pretty similar jobs and there are also multiple different types of public notaries so make sure that you look into each of these different opportunities.

Basically what a public notary does is they watch other people assign legal documents and then they generally sign the document themselves as well to notarize it and make it completely official public notaries charge anywhere between about ten dollars and a hundred and fifty dollars per signing that they witness although they don't get to set their own rates it depends on what state you live in and what type of signing it is.

Now this might sound like a really technical job that you're definitely not qualified to do but it's actually really easy to become a public notary in order to find out exactly how just google your state's name and then how to become a public notary so for example I live in Oregon so I would search something like how to become a public notary in Oregon but in most states the process is pretty similar first you take a short course and buy a short course I mean of course that takes you several hours not several years once you've taken the course then you pay a fee to take an examination and if you pass then you become a certified public notary in your state.

5) personal trainer:

Let's move on to the next job on my list and that is personal trainer now a personal trainer can work in a gym for a gym or they can be a freelance personal trainer or they can even work online and work directly with clients over Skype or zoom or some other video conferencing software like that.

You probably already know what a personal trainer does they help people learn how to exercise they design workout programs for people to follow in order to get in shape and lose weight.

Personal trainers can earn anywhere from around twenty-five dollars all the way up to about a hundred dollars per hour although most seem to earn in that 50 to 70 dollar an hour range.

Job number six on my list is that of motion graphics designer so this is someone that designs the words or graphics that you see in videos and this person might also edit videos or they might only specialize in designing these motion graphics.

7) app developer :

The next site has little opportunity on the list is that of app developer now in order to become an app developer you will have to learn a few coding languages as well as the skills to develop the apps themselves you can learn most of these skills by taking online courses or you can choose to take part in an intensive app development bootcamp these programs typically lasts anywhere from about 12 weeks all the way up to six months and while you're in them they are a full-time job.

App developers typically earn around $100,000 per year so you could do it full-time or if you want to do it as a side has alt then it would all depend on how many hours you actually want to put into developing apps.

8) small business marketing agency :

The next side has the opportunity is to start your own small business marketing agency whether the economy is up or the economy is down there is always demand for help with marketing and that's because when the economy is up businesses have more money and they want for that money into marketing so that they can grow their businesses even more rapidly.

On the other hand when the economy is down they reach out to marketers for help because they know that they desperately need more customers with so many people starting their own businesses over the past few years there are literally hundreds of thousands if not millions of small businesses in need of help finding customers. marketing agencies typically design custom packages for each of their clients and the client pays per month for that package so the package for example might include advertising on Facebook and Instagram or it might include video marketing on YouTube it might also include help with their websites, SEO or all sorts of other digital marketing services.
